(average velocity)			平均速度
a(acceleration)				加速度
t(time)						时间

acceleration	n.	加速，加快；加速度

displacement = (initial velocity + final velocity) / 2 * time
final velocity = initial velocity + acceleration * time
displacement = initial velocity * time + acceleration * time * time / 2
displacement = final velocity * time - acceleration * time * time / 2
final acceleration * final acceleration = initial velocity * initial velocity + 2 * acceleration * time
average velocity = displacement / time = (initial velocity + final velocity) / 2
